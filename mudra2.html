CLASSICAL TRADITIONS
Dance Forms

Major Bharatiya Natya traditions that employ mudras as a sacred language

💃

BHARATANATYAM

TAMIL NADU · SOUTH INDIA

The oldest classical dance, rooted in the Devadasi tradition. Mudras here are Hastas — the alphabet of Abhinaya.

🤲 28 ASAMYUTA + 24 SAMYUTA
🌀

KATHAK

UTTAR PRADESH · NORTH INDIA

Rooted in North Indian storytelling, blending Hindu devotion with Mughal court aesthetics. Mudras are fluid and narrative.

🤲 66+ CLASSICAL HASTAS
🌸

ODISSI

ODISHA · EAST INDIA

Rooted in temple sculptures, known for lyrical grace and richly expressive mudras tied to Krishna devotion.

🤲 20+ PRIMARY HASTAS
